The Glomar Denial: A Spy's Tale

Dr. Allison Hale was a child therapist, trained to understand the minds of the vulnerable. Recruited into IBM, she develops a close working relationship with Jude, a brilliant scientist. She doesn’t realize she met him years before as her client. Drawn into a world of chasing predators, Allison believes she is studying them scientifically—until the script flips, and she becomes the prey. This sets the scene for my novel The Glomar Denial: A Spy's Tale.

Inspired by my experience as a child therapist, work in financial crime, and anti-human trafficking research, this thriller examines how elite criminal networks operate in plain sight, leveraging technology, corporate influence, and financial systems.

Readers of Red Sparrow or The Girl with the Dragon Tattoo will enjoy this story, which blends intelligence operations, behavioral science, artificial intelligence, and a Wuthering Heights-style romance set in Utah.
