The Glomar Denial: A Novel by Dawn Marie Daras

Sub-title: A Novel that Demonstrates How Elite Trafficking Moves Through the Corporate World

About the Book

Dr. Allison Hale was a child therapist, trained to understand the minds of the vulnerable. Recruited into IBM, she develops a close working relationship with Jude, a brilliant scientist from BYU. She does not realize that she has met him years earlier, as a client. Drawn into a world of chasing predators, she believes she is scientifically studying them—until the script is flipped, and she becomes the prey. This sets the scene for The Glomar Denial.

The novel explores how elite human trafficking networks operate through corporate and financial systems, blending behavioral science, artificial intelligence, and intelligence operations. It also contains a Wuthering Heights-style romance and is set in Utah. Fans of Red Sparrow or The Girl with the Dragon Tattoo will find it engaging and thrilling.
